How It Works

Bend Cut Send provides laser-cut metal parts based on your own designs. Our process is intentionally simple â€“ you send us a drawing, choose the material and finish, and we deliver accurately cut parts.

1. Prepare your design

Start by preparing a 2D or 3D design of the parts you want to have cut. We accept a range of common formats typically exported from CAD software, including DWG, DXF, DWT, DWS, DWF, DWFx, DXB, PDF, STL and common image formats (JPEG, PNG, TIFF, BMP). Please ensure that:

  • Dimensions are clearly indicated and drawn at 1:1 scale where possible.
  • All parts to be cut are visible on the drawing and not hidden by layers.
  • Holes and cutouts are sized appropriately for the material thickness.

2. Choose material, finish and thickness

On the Request a Quote page, you will select a material, surface finish and thickness combination. These options are limited to combinations we can cut reliably. The dropdowns on the form guide you through valid choices.

If you are unsure which material or thickness is best for your application, you can use the Contact Us/Ask a Question page to ask for advice before submitting a quote request.

3. Submit a quote request

Once your design file is ready and you know the material requirements, complete the quote form with your contact details and address. Upload your design file and specify the quantity required. You can also add comments if there is anything special we should know about your project.

After you submit the form, you will receive an automatic confirmation email summarising your request. At the same time, we receive your details and design so that we can prepare a quotation.

4. Receive your quotation

We will review your design, check the selected material and thickness, and calculate cutting time and material usage. If anything is unclear or needs adjustment, we may contact you by email to confirm details before finalising the quote.

Once ready, we send you a quotation by email. The quotation will include pricing, lead time estimates and any notes or recommendations.

5. Production and delivery

When you accept the quotation and confirm that you would like to proceed, we schedule your job for cutting. Your parts are then laser cut according to your design and the agreed material specification.

After cutting, your order is packed for delivery. Depending on your location and our arrangement with you, we either ship the parts to your address or make them available for collection.

Need help?

If any part of this process is unclear, or you are not sure if your design or file format is suitable, please send us a message via the Contact Us/Ask a Question page. We are happy to help.
